Home For Sale in Sammamish, Washington (Sammamish Plateau)

Dec 28, 2006

We’re selling our home.  

As I mentioned in [my 5 things about me post][1], we’ve decided to look for a larger place to make things more comfortable when my parents come in town to visit.  Although I am excited about the possibilities in getting a larger place, it will be hard to say goodbye to this place.  We are the original owners, buying the home new in the fall of 2000.  This is the only home Josie has known and we’ve performed countless upgrades to the home over the years.

If you are looking for a great home on the Sammamish Plateau with a 30 minute commute to Microsoft, drop me a line.  You can see all the pictures [in a Flickr Set][2].
