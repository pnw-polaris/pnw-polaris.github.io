An update from Lenn Pryor

Jun 26, 2005

Powerful blog post from Lenn:

We have decided to consider making Tallinn home, but have a little more thinking to sort out. Watching a country with a great and deep history and culture emerge from 50 years of soviet occupation is something quite amazing to see. The people are smart, well educated, hard working, kind if not a bit distant. They are also stunningly beautiful as a whole. It is a unique place with a unique culture and a unique language reshaping itself in a new era. I love a leaf turning moment, the chance to redefine. Nothing is more invigorating than starting over with new rules and perspectives, shaking off the funk of complaceny.

We are going through this ourselves as a family so it seems somehow appropriate to be here. Life is constant change for us, a new son, a new job, a new location, a new way of life, a new language. So much change to absorb, so much to digest, reflect, consume and move on. Much like Estonia, much like Tallinn, much like Skype. Nothing in life is permanent but change. Welcome to changeville.

Awesome post Lenn, from the heart. We hope you, Melissa and Blue are doing great, sounds like you are.
