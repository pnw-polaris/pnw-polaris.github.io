The Mix BlogZone

Apr 20, 2007

During Mix my team will be filming a variety of content to cover the event.  If you’re following along at home you’ll be able to watch this all on the [Mix Community site][1]. We’ll have interviews with partners who are building cool stuff, product team interviews/demos and conversations with attendees.  It can be challenging to get decent bandwith to upload content like this while at an even so we’ll be using the Mix BlogZone as our HQ.   We’ll conduct some interviews there too, should be fun.

So what’s the BlogZone?  It is a place for bloggers to hang out at Mix, upload content and have some food.  (I saw the menu the other day and its nice comfort food).   The plan in addition to comfort food is to provide good connectivity, drinks, XBox gaming and plasma screens so you can watch the keynotes and blog in real time.  

The BlogZone will be on the 3rd floor of the Venetian Tower (look for signage – its the main tower where the rooms are, away from the main conference area) and will open up Sunday night at 9:00 pm.  Following that we’ll have it open at 8:00 am each day during the conference.

See you there!  (Back to work, my hair is on fire as usual)
